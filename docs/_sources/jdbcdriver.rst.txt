JDBC Drivers
============

Jaqy requires `JDBC drivers <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/JDBC_driver>`__ to function.
You will need to download them separately.  Here are some links that you may find useful.

+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Database          | LINK                                                                                   |
+===================+========================================================================================+
| Apache Derby      | https://db.apache.org/derby/                                                           |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| MySQL             | https://www.mysql.com/products/connector/                                              |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| PostgreSQL        | https://jdbc.postgresql.org/                                                           |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| SQL Server        | https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/sql/connect/jdbc/microsoft-jdbc-driver-for-sql-server |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| SQLite            | https://github.com/xerial/sqlite-jdbc                                                  |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Teradata          | https://downloads.teradata.com/download/connectivity/jdbc-driver                       |
+-------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
